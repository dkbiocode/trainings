## First Steps After Launch

Once your Codespace launches (takes about 30 seconds):

### 1. Orient Yourself

Follow the [System Setup Guide](../system-setup-guide.html) to understand your environment:
- Where am I? (understanding directories)
- What tools are available?
- How many CPUs do I have?

### 2. Choose Your Path

**New to command line?**
Start with [Shell for Beginners](../shell-beginner/)

**Ready for bioinformatics?**
Jump to [Metagenomics Lessons](../metagenomics/)

### 3. Keep Learning

Work through the lessons at your own pace. You can:
- Pause and resume your Codespace anytime
- Stop it when taking breaks to save hours
- Delete it when done to free storage

## Important Tips

⚠️ **Stop your Codespace when not using it!**
- Codespaces continue running (using hours) even when you close the browser
- Go to [github.com/codespaces](https://github.com/codespaces) and click "Stop codespace"
- Set auto-timeout: Settings → Codespaces → 30 minutes recommended

✅ **Monitor your usage**
- Check remaining hours at [github.com/settings/billing](https://github.com/settings/billing)
- Free tier: 120 core-hours/month (60 hours on 2-core machine)
